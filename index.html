GFR Stages & Disease Progression

  • Stage 1: GFR ≥ 90 — Normal or high kidney function.
  • Stage 2: GFR 60–89 — Mild kidney damage.
  • Stage 3: GFR 30–59 — Moderate kidney damage.
  • Stage 4: GFR 15–29 — Severe kidney damage.
  • Stage 5: GFR < 15 — Kidney failure (dialysis/transplant needed).

Note: Disease progression often occurs gradually. Early detection and consistent monitoring can delay or prevent progression.
